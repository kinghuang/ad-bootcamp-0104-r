* [an ordered collection of the same type](http://cran.r-project.org/doc/manuals/R-intro.html#Vectors-and-assignment)

		> c(1, 2, 3)
		[1] 1 2 3
		> c("a", "bb", "ccc", "dddd")
		[1] "a" "bb" "ccc" "dddd"
		> c(1, "a", TRUE)
		[1] "1" "a" "TRUE"
		> a = c(1, 2, 3)
		> b = c(10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 60)
		> a + b
		[1] 11 22 33 41 52 63
